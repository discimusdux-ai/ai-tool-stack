## The 5 Categories of AI Video Tools

Before comparing tools, understand the category:

1.  **Avatar Video** (HeyGen, Synthesia) — AI presenter videos without cameras. Ideal for training and sales.
2.  **AI Video Generation** (Runway, Sora, Kling) — Generate footage from text or images. Ideal for B-roll and creative ads.

4.  **Short-Form / Social** (CapCut, Opus Clip) — TikTok, Reels, Shorts optimization and auto-clipping.
5.  **Video Translation** (HeyGen, Papercup) — Dub videos into other languages with lip-sync.

## 2. Runway — Best for Generative Video & VFX

Runway is the creative professional's playground. With the release of Gen-4.5, Runway has moved beyond "dreamy" AI visuals into stable, controllable cinematography. It is the tool of choice for agencies that need to generate B-roll without a camera crew.

### 5+ Key Features
*   **Gen-4.5 Alpha**: Text-to-video with unprecedented temporal consistency (no more flickering).
*   **Motion Brush**: Paint over an area of a still image to dictate exactly where movement should occur.
*   **Act-One**: Use your own facial performance via webcam to drive the expressions of a 3D or AI character.
*   **Advanced Inpainting**: Remove complex moving objects from a scene with one click.
*   **Multi-Motion Control**: Independently control camera pan, tilt, and zoom within a generated clip.
*   **Custom Model Training**: Train the AI on your specific brand style or product.

### Specific Cons
*   **Steep Learning Curve**: The "Director Mode" requires an understanding of camera focal lengths and prompt engineering.
*   **Generation Time**: High-quality 4K generations can take 2-4 minutes per 10-second clip during peak hours.
*   **Credit Expiration**: On the Standard plan, unused credits do not always roll over, forcing a "use it or lose it" workflow.

### Pricing (June 2026)

*   **Standard**: $15/mo — 625 credits/month, 720p, 5 active projects.
*   **Pro**: $35/mo — 2,250 credits/month, 4K, 50 projects.

## 3. Descript — Best for Podcast & YouTube Editing

Descript has evolved from a simple transcription tool into a full-stack "Under-the-hood" AI editor. The core philosophy remains: if you can edit a Word document, you can edit a video.

### 5+ Key Features
*   **Underdub & Overdub**: Fix a typo in your speech by typing the correct word; the AI generates it in your voice.
*   **Regenerative Blur**: Automatically blurs backgrounds or sensitive information in screen recordings.
*   **Studio Sound 2.0**: Makes a cheap laptop mic sound like a $500 Shure SM7B.
*   **Eye Contact Correction**: Uses AI to make it look like you are looking at the lens even if you are reading a script.
*   **Filler Word Removal**: Instantly deletes "um," "uh," and "like" from both the transcript and the video.

### Specific Cons
*   **Resource Heavy**: The desktop app is notorious for high RAM usage, often slowing down mid-range laptops.
*   **Transcription Errors**: While 95% accurate, technical jargon or heavy accents still require significant manual cleanup.
*   **Limited Color Grading**: It is not a replacement for DaVinci Resolve; professional colorists will find the tools lacking.

### Pricing (June 2026)
*   **Free**: 1 hour transcription/month, 720p, 1 remote recording.
*   **Creator**: $24/mo — 10 hours transcription, 4K, Overdub, Studio Sound.
*   **Business**: $40/mo — 40 hours, multiple users, team collaboration.
*   **Enterprise**: Custom pricing with dedicated support.

## Who Is It For? (Use Case Mapping)

Choosing the right tool depends entirely on your output goals:

*   **The Solopreneur / YouTuber**: Use **Descript** for your main editing and **CapCut** for your Shorts. This combo is the most efficient for a one-person team.
*   **The Sales Leader**: Use **HeyGen**. The ability to send 500 personalized "Hey [Name]" videos in an afternoon is a game-changer for outbound response rates.
*   **The Creative Director**: Use **Runway**. When you need a specific visual that doesn't exist in stock libraries, Runway's generative tools are the only solution.
*   **The HR / L&D Manager**: Use **Synthesia**. The SCORM compliance and professional avatar library make it the only viable choice for large-scale corporate training.
*   **The Social Media Manager**: Use **CapCut** and **Opus Clip**. These tools are built for the "fast-twitch" nature of social algorithms.
