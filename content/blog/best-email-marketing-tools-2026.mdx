## 1. Mailchimp — Best for Small Businesses Getting Started

Mailchimp is still the most-used email platform with 11M+ customers. Its **free plan gives you 500 contacts and 1,000 sends/month** — enough to test email marketing before committing.

**Where Mailchimp shines:**
- Drag-and-drop builder with 100+ templates
- AI-powered subject line and content suggestions
- Audience insights and basic segmentation
- 300+ integrations including Shopify, WooCommerce, Salesforce
- Predictive demographics (estimates age/gender for smarter targeting)

**The honest downsides:**
- **Counts unsubscribed contacts** toward your limit — your bill grows even when people opt out
- Automation is limited compared to ActiveCampaign; complex sequences require workarounds
- Price jumps are steep: from 500 to 50,000 contacts, you go from $13/mo to $350/mo

**Verdict**: Ideal starting point for local businesses, restaurants, and small e-commerce. Once you need multi-step automations or hit 2K+ contacts, evaluate ConvertKit or ActiveCampaign.

> **Try Mailchimp**: [Free up to 500 contacts →](/tools/mailchimp)

## 2. ConvertKit — Best for Creators and Newsletter Builders

ConvertKit was purpose-built for creators: bloggers, YouTubers, podcasters, course sellers, and newsletter writers. Its tag-based subscriber model is more flexible than Mailchimp's list-based approach.

**What makes ConvertKit different:**
- **Tag-based segmentation**: Every subscriber gets tags based on what they clicked, purchased, or opted into — no separate lists to manage
- **Visual automation builder**: Create complex sequences visually — if/then logic, wait steps, tag triggers
- **Creator Network**: Built-in recommendation network to cross-promote with other newsletters (free subscriber growth)
- **Commerce**: Sell digital products and courses directly from ConvertKit with no transaction fees
- **Deliverability**: Consistently rated #1 or #2 in deliverability across industry tests

**Pricing**: Free up to 1,000 subscribers (limited automation). Creator plan at $25/mo (1K subscribers) unlocks full automations. Scales to $119/mo at 50K subscribers.

**Verdict**: The best platform if you're building an audience-first business — newsletter, course, coaching, or content brand. The free plan to 1,000 is the most generous in the category.

> **Try ConvertKit**: [Free up to 1,000 subscribers →](/tools/convertkit)

## 3. ActiveCampaign — Best for E-Commerce and B2B Automation

ActiveCampaign is what you upgrade to when Mailchimp's automations feel too limited. Its visual automation builder handles workflows that would require expensive workarounds elsewhere.

**Automation examples unique to ActiveCampaign:**
- Customer buys Product A → 3 days later, email about related Product B → if no open, send SMS reminder → if purchase, move to VIP segment
- Lead fills out demo request → score increases → if score >50, create CRM deal and assign to sales rep → if no activity in 7 days, automated follow-up
- Cart abandoned → 1-hour email → 24-hour email with discount → 72-hour email final offer → remove from sequence on purchase

**Standout features:**
- **CRM with deal pipelines** built into the same platform
- **Lead scoring** based on email behavior + site tracking + custom events
- **Predictive sending** — ML determines optimal send time per subscriber
- **Site personalization** — show different website content based on subscriber data
- **800+ integrations** including Shopify, WooCommerce, Salesforce, Zapier

**Pricing**: Starts at $15/mo for 1,000 contacts. Plus plan ($49/mo) adds CRM features and SMS. No free plan — 14-day trial only.

**Verdict**: Best-in-class for revenue-driving automation. Typical ActiveCampaign user sees 20-40% email revenue lift from behavioral triggers vs basic blast campaigns.

> **Try ActiveCampaign**: [14-day free trial →](/tools/activecampaign)

## 4. Klaviyo — Best for E-Commerce Stores Specifically

If you're running a Shopify or WooCommerce store, Klaviyo deserves special mention. It's purpose-built for e-commerce with pre-built flows that generate revenue from day one.

**Klaviyo e-commerce flows (set up in less than 1 hour):**
- Abandoned cart (avg 15% recovery rate)
- Welcome series (5x higher engagement than cold sends)
- Post-purchase (review requests, upsells)
- Browse abandonment
- Win-back for dormant customers
- VIP segment rewards

**Pricing**: Free up to 250 contacts (500 emails/mo). Scales to $20/mo at 500 contacts, $45/mo at 1K contacts.

**Vs ActiveCampaign**: Klaviyo is narrower (e-commerce only) but deeper for Shopify. ActiveCampaign is better for mixed B2B/B2C or SaaS businesses.

## 5. Beehiiv — Best Free Option for Newsletter Publishers

Beehiiv entered the market in 2022 and rapidly became the go-to for serious newsletter publishers. The free plan has **no subscriber limit** — you can grow to 2,500 subscribers for free, then pay $42/mo.

**What Beehiiv offers:**
- Built-in referral program (incentivize subscribers to share)
- Ad network (get paid for ad placements once you hit scale)
- Segmentation and analytics comparable to paid Mailchimp plans
- Custom domain and clean web archive of past issues
- No template needed — clean email-first design by default

**Best for**: Anyone building a newsletter as a primary product. Not ideal for e-commerce or complex automations.

## Which Tool Should You Choose?

**Choose Mailchimp if:**
- You're just starting (the free plan gives room to learn)
- You're a local business or non-profit with basic newsletter needs
- You send occasional campaigns (not complex automations)

**Choose ConvertKit if:**
- You're a creator, blogger, or newsletter publisher
- You sell digital products or courses
- You care about deliverability and clean list hygiene
- You want to monetize your audience through recommendations

**Choose ActiveCampaign if:**
- You run an e-commerce store with behavioral triggers
- You have a B2B sales process that email should support
- You need email + CRM in one platform
- You're generating >$50K/year and want automation to scale revenue

**Choose Klaviyo if:**
- 100% of your business is Shopify or WooCommerce
- You want the deepest e-commerce email integration available

## FAQ

**What's the best email marketing tool for beginners?**
Mailchimp — the free plan works for up to 500 contacts with no credit card needed. The drag-and-drop builder and template library make it easy to send professional emails in 30 minutes.

**Which email platform has the best deliverability?**
ConvertKit and ActiveCampaign consistently rank #1-2 in deliverability tests. Both maintain strict list hygiene policies and shared IP reputations. Mailchimp is solid but can be affected by lower-quality senders on shared IPs.

**How much does email marketing cost for 10,000 subscribers?**
Mailchimp: ~$110/mo. ConvertKit: ~$119/mo. ActiveCampaign: ~$99/mo. Klaviyo: ~$150/mo. All are within a similar range at scale — the decision comes down to features and automation depth, not price.

**Can I switch email platforms without losing subscribers?**
Yes — you can export your subscriber list as CSV from any platform and import it to another. Most platforms have migration guides. You'll lose automation history and contact activity data, but subscribers transfer cleanly. Notify your list before switching to maintain trust.

**Is email marketing still worth it in 2026?**
Email delivers $36 ROI for every $1 spent — consistently the highest of any marketing channel. Social media algorithms reduce your reach; with email you own the audience. As AI-driven spam increases, curated email lists become more valuable, not less.
