# Best AI Video Tools in 2026

Video dominates every platform — YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, even email. But producing professional video traditionally requires cameras, lighting, editing skills, and hours of post-production.

AI video tools in 2026 collapse that entire pipeline. Text-to-video, AI avatars, automatic editing, instant subtitles, smart repurposing — here's what's worth using.

## Categories of AI Video Tools

The video space is broad, so we've organized tools by what they do best:

- **AI Video Generation** — Create video from text/images
- **AI Editing** — Automated cuts, subtitles, effects
- **AI Avatars** — Talking-head videos without filming
- **AI Repurposing** — Turn long content into short clips

## AI Video Generation

### Runway (Gen-3 Alpha) — Best AI Video Generation

Runway's Gen-3 Alpha model produces the most coherent AI-generated video available to consumers. Text-to-video, image-to-video, and video-to-video transformations are all production-viable.

**Best for:** Marketers needing B-roll, creative agencies, concept visualization

**Key features:**
- Text-to-video (10-second clips, extendable)
- Image-to-video (animate any still image)
- Motion brush (control where movement happens)
- Video inpainting (remove/replace objects in footage)
- Upscaling and frame interpolation

**Pricing:** Free tier available. Standard at $15/month (625 credits).

### HeyGen — Best for AI Avatar Videos

HeyGen creates talking-head videos using AI avatars. Upload a script, pick an avatar (or create one from your own likeness), and get a polished video in minutes.

**Best for:** Training videos, product demos, personalized outreach

**Key features:**
- 200+ stock AI avatars
- Custom avatar creation (from a few minutes of footage)
- 40+ languages with lip-sync
- Script-to-video in under 5 minutes
- Brand kit (logos, colors, templates)

**Pricing:** Free (1 credit/month). Creator at $29/month.

## AI Video Editing

### Descript — Best for Podcast and Video Editing

Descript's "edit video like a document" approach is genuinely revolutionary. It transcribes your video, and you edit by editing the text. Delete a sentence from the transcript, and the video cut happens automatically.

**Best for:** Podcasters, YouTube creators, anyone who films talking-head content

**Key features:**
- Text-based video editing (edit transcript = edit video)
- AI filler word removal (um, uh, like)
- Eye contact correction (makes you look at the camera)
- Studio Sound (AI audio enhancement)
- AI voice cloning (fix mistakes without re-recording)
- Screen recording with webcam overlay

**Pricing:** Free (limited). Hobbyist at $8/month. Business at $33/month.

**Verdict:** If you create any talking-head content, Descript saves hours per video. The text-based editing paradigm is faster than timeline-based editing for 90% of use cases.

### CapCut — Best Free AI Video Editor

CapCut (by ByteDance) packs an absurd amount of AI features into a free tool. Auto-captions, background removal, style transfer, AI-generated effects — all free, all good quality.

**Best for:** Social media creators, TikTok/Reels/Shorts creators, budget-conscious editors

**Key features:**
- Auto-captions with animated styles
- AI background removal
- AI upscaling and frame interpolation
- Text-to-speech with natural voices
- One-click style effects
- Desktop, mobile, and web versions

**Pricing:** Free (with watermark on some features). Pro at $8/month.

## AI Video Repurposing

### Opus Clip — Best for Repurposing Long Videos into Shorts

Opus Clip takes a long video (podcast, webinar, stream) and automatically identifies the most compelling moments, creates short clips, adds captions, and formats for vertical platforms.

**Best for:** Podcasters, webinar hosts, YouTube creators wanting short-form content

**Key features:**
- AI identifies viral-worthy clips automatically
- Auto-captions with customizable styles
- Auto-reframe (horizontal to vertical)
- Virality score for each clip
- Batch processing (multiple clips from one video)

**Pricing:** Free (limited). Starter at $19/month.

**Verdict:** If you create long-form content and want to repurpose it for TikTok/Reels/Shorts, Opus Clip pays for itself immediately. What would take 2-3 hours of manual editing happens in minutes.

### Vizard — Best for Webinar and Meeting Repurposing

Vizard is specifically optimized for turning professional content (webinars, meetings, presentations) into polished short clips. Better for B2B content than Opus Clip.

**Best for:** B2B marketers, SaaS companies, conference speakers

**Key features:**
- AI clip detection optimized for professional content
- Speaker detection and auto-framing
- Brand template system
- Bulk editing across multiple clips
- Direct publish to social platforms

**Pricing:** Free (limited). Pro at $30/month.

## Building a Video Workflow

Here's a practical workflow using these tools:

1. **Record** your core content (podcast, webinar, talking-head)
2. **Edit** in Descript (remove filler, fix mistakes, add polish)
3. **Repurpose** with Opus Clip (generate 10-15 short clips)
4. **Enhance** clips in CapCut (captions, effects, transitions)
5. **Generate** supplementary content with Runway/HeyGen (intros, B-roll, explainers)

This workflow turns one hour of raw content into weeks of multi-platform video without a production team.
